Kashmir · Winter · 1964

There was a love story.

In the snow-kissed valleys of Kashmir, amid the echoes of distant gunfire and the silence between battles, a soldier with no family received a letter from a stranger — a woman who called herself his wife.

He did not know her face. He did not know her name. But he knew, with every beat of his soldier's heart, that he would find her, wherever destiny had hidden her.

One Letter. Across Time.

Twenty years later. A young woman named Afreen is tasked with delivering a single letter from Ram to Sita. She does not know what the letter holds. She does not know that finding Sita will unravel the most beautiful love story she has ever heard.

Some love stories are not meant to be witnessed by those who live them — they are meant to be discovered by those who come after.
